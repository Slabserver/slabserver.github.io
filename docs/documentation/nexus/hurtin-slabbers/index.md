# Hurtin' Slabbers

Hurtin' Slabbers by maketues. It started as a survival build on Slab. The Nexus version keeps the game identical but adds instancing, protected blocks, and a proper save system so progress carries between sessions.

![hurtin slabbers](../../../assets/images/nexus/hurtin-slabbers.png)



# How to Play

## The Game

Hurtin' Slabbers is a linear platformer, each level a series of obstacles that tests your Minecraft movement skills. There is a leaderboard to track your time and compete against other players, and most levels have more than one route through them.

There are 8 levels in total, ending in a boss fight. Completing the whole game takes around 90 minutes depending on skill. You do not need to finish it in one go.

## Checkpoints

Checkpoints sit between levels. When you reach one, click the sign and THEN stand on the campfire to die. You should see a message to confirm it worked.

If you die, you respawn at your last checkpoint.

## Saving and Quitting

You do not need to finish in one session. The server will remember what level you were on and your time and will put you back at the checkpoint (as if you died).
