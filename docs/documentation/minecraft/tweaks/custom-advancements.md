# Custom Advancements

Slabserver features custom Minecraft advancements in order to gradually onboard new players to our community server, guiding them towards important server locations and our custom gameplay elements.

## Welcome
| Name | Description | Notes |
|------|-------------|-------|
| **Slabserver** | Join the Etho Community Server
| **Getting Started** | Visit the Town Hall at the center of Spawn
| **Knowledge is Power** | Read the guide in Town Hall on how to play on Slabserver |

## Spawn & Shopping District

| Name | Description | Notes |
|------|-------------|-------|
| **Shopping Spree** | Visit the Shopping District toward the northeast of Spawn |
| **Sudo.. Sumo... Shroomo... Spawn?** | Find the original worldspawn location where Season 4 began, known as 'Pseudospawn'... or 'Shroomospawn' |
| **Spawntown Scenic Sights** | Explore Spawn, and visit the Build Memorial, Slab Mountain's summit, the northern armor trim huts, the eastern Mail Room by the docks, and the southwest Spawn Plaza |
| **Spawntown Social Spots** | Visit the Death Games, the Elytra Course starting room, the PVP arena inside Slab Mountain, the Chess board, and the Court Room and Conference Room in City Hall |
| **Spawntown Suggestions** | Take part in the Resource World Naming Poll at Spawn |

### 'Here, There, Over Where? Everywhere!?!? ...and Back Again' Criteria

| Name | Description | Notes |
